All About The Goalie

In soccer, goalie is the last line of defence. Goalie is the only player on the team that is allowed to use their hands. When the opposing offence shoots the ball and it gets past the defense it's the goalie job to stop it before it goes in the goal. Goalie is one the hardest positions to play because not many people want to play it and whenever the opposing team scores you are the first to blame. It's not easy to play goalie but without one there whould be no team.
