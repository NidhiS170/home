All About Defense

In soccer, the defenders stay back and protect the goal. When th opposing team offence gets the ball it is the defense's job to keep them from scoring. The defence stay in between the ball and the goal so the offence can't shoot. The defence have to try there best to take the ball from the opposition before they score a goal.
