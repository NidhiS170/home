All About Offence

In soccer, offence score the goals. Offenders take the ball and try to get past the opposing teams defence to score a goal. There are 3 kinds of offenders: right winger, left winger, and striker. Striker stays in the middle and the wingers are on either side of him. Together they pass the ball until they are close enough to shoot it, or kick it, into the opposing goal. At the end of the game, the team that scores the most goals win.
